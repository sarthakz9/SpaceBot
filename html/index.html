TECHTALK

Date : 11th November 2017

Venue : LT-5 , Jaypee Institute of Information Technology,Sector 62,Noida

Sanya Khurana is a Software Developer at Adobe Systems, India. She is also the Founder and Chapter Leader of Lean In India - a community of 66 Lean In Circles and 1700 members that empowers women towards their ambitions. She is a post-graduate and also interned in Adobe and IBM. She is a proud recipient of Google Women Techmakers Scholarship 2016, Facebook Grace Hopper Conference Scholarship 2016 and Grace Hopper Conference India Scholarship 2015. She has created Android/iOS Mobile Application, has been an open source contributor and also mentored at Learn-IT Girl.

She will talk about Starting Lean In Circles, about all the scholarships in tech, and her journey in the CS field being a non-btech student, how she overcame her self doubts and fears along the way.

Diksha Deo, a IOT enthusiast and full stack developer working primarily in Python and node.js. She is a constant contributor to WomenWhoCode, HeadStart, Google WomenTechMaker as a Speaker, Lead and Member. She readily takes any opportunity which caters her vision of helping people. She shares the belief that things you own shouldn't own you, that you should be free to change and make them truly yours. Her endless curiosity and enthusiasm to learn how everything works makes Diksha a "pro" in uniting digital and physical worlds. Co-Founder and Director at Ckho, Founder and CEO at Incubsence.
